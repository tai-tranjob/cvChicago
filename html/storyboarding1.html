THE VESSEL (PROCESSING)

Storyboard Pro (2024)

A mid-term work from my Visualization and Storyboarding class during Spring semester 2024. The Vessel is an adventure of a mysterious female character searching for her desired target, a monster with the body of a dinosaur but the face of a dog.

This is my first project that unlocks new skills: learnt how to use Storyboard Pro, came up with multiple designs for the monster, completed beat boards, storyboards and final animatic with sounds.
